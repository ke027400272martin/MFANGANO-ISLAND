How to find us

  • Open the Mfangano webpage on your phone.
  • Select HISTORY to know the history of Mfangano Island.
  • Explore Mfangano Island.
  • Help develop Mfangano Island.

How to buy fish

  • Visit: www.mfanganofisheries.co.ke
  • Select: Buy fish
  • Choose the type of fish you want to buy.
  • Enter the amount and your address and it will be delivered to you.

Mobile Money Safety Tips

  • Never share your M-PESA PIN with anyone.
  • Use a strong, hard-to-guess PIN.
